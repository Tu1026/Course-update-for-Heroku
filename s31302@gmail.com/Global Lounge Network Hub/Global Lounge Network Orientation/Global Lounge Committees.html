Committees

As you know, part the club requirements is that the Global Lounge Officer sits on a committee to plan Global Lounge Programming. The committees are as follows:

Intercultural Committee strives to delve beyond superficial interactions with culture for deeper understanding and intercultural learning

Sustainability Committee focuses on sustainability efforts as they pertain to global citizenship and on how it intersects with other issues social justice

Anti-racism Committee engages with conversations on how to dismantle racists systems and build a more just and equitable society

International Development Committee focuses on bringing a critical lens to conversations of international development

Feeling torn?

All of these topic are deeply connected so don’t worry if you find your interests divided among them – one committee will always be examining topics relevant to another. What is most important to us is that each of you is working on something that excites you and you are passionate about

Once the committees are formed, you can connect with resources, discussion and the rest of your committee members in the Committee Spaces (in development):
