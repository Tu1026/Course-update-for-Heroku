Welcome to the network

We acknowledge that the Global Lounge and UBC are located on the traditional, ancestral, unceded homelands of the hən̓q̓əmin̓əm̓-speaking xʷməθkwəy̓əm (Musqueam) people, the People of the Grassy River.  This has always been a place of learning for xʷməθkwəy̓əm people, and we hope to honor that history.

We understand that not everyone is coming to xʷməθkwəy̓əm territory this year.  You can find out more about the indigenous territory in other parts of the world here.

The Global Lounge is here to build capacity for students to see their ideas come to fruition and effect positive change. You are now part of a wide network of other student clubs and organizations working toward many common and intersecting goals with their leadership, advocacy and activism.  The Global Lounge Network Hub is exclusive to network members – for our public events and wider programming visit the Global Lounge Community Hub!
