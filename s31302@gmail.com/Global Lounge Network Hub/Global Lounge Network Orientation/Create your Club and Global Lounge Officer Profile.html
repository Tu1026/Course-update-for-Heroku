We want to showcase the people and organizations that make up the Global Lounge Network and help connect with each other.  

Please upload a word document that includes:

 

  • Your club/organization logo
  • Club/organization profile (max 250 words)
  • Things your club is interested in connecting about
  • Contacts, website, social media, etc...
  • Global Lounge Officer photo
  • Global Lounge Officer profile (max 250 words)
  • Things you are interested in connecting on
  • Contact information

You only need to submit one assignment per club, so Presidents and Global Lounge Officers, please coordinate who will submit on your clubs behalf.
